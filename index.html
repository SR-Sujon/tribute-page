William Shakespeare

An English playwright, poet and actor

Here's a time line of William Shakespeare's life:

  • 1564 - William Shakespeare is born in Stratford-upon-Avon, England.
  • 1582 - Shakespeare marries Anne Hathaway, who is eight years older than him, and they have three children together.
  • 1590-1613 - Shakespeare writes many of his famous plays, including Romeo and Juliet, Hamlet, Macbeth, Othello, and King Lear.
  • 1603 - Shakespeare's company is renamed the King's Men after King James I takes the throne.
  • 1609 - Shakespeare's sonnets are published, which become some of his most famous poetry.
  • 1613 - The Globe Theatre burns down during a performance of Henry VIII, but is later rebuilt.
  • 1616 - Shakespeare dies in Stratford-upon-Avon at the age of 52, and is buried in Holy Trinity Church.
  • 1623 - Shakespeare's friends and fellow actors publish the First Folio, a collection of his plays, which becomes a significant literary work.
